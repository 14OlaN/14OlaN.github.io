Wallpaper catalogue for Laine + Alliage

Brooklyn, NY, 2023

The size, slightly larger than an A5 notebook, makes the catalogue perfect for distribution at events and through mail. There is an emphasis on craft and the designer's care and process shown in the handwritten text and sketches, provided by the designer. The brand's ease and airy feel carry through the booklet. The catalogue ends with a guide for placing orders that explains the product terminology and specifications. I also assisted in photoshoots for this catalogue, staging and creating compositions, and providing technical support.

Brooklyn, NY, 2024

Manifesto for Utopia

2024

What does ChatGPT think Utopia is like? The manifesto outlines the structures of utopian life and how humanity can reach it. The text is written by ChatGPT, and edited and prompted by me. I wrote the last chapter to provide context and biases of ChatGPT. Illustrations and calligraphy by me. Hand-bound with laser-engraved leather.

FOLIO Magazine

in progress

FOLIO is a fictional art & culture magazine with which I reimagined the digital reading experience. I propose that websites can democratize access to information; promote more intentional, critical, and convenient reading experiences; and offer an editorial experience that complements rather than copies print.

FOLIO's interactivity encourages closer reading and signals that the magazine knows its readers are digitally fluent. Personalisation (like saving notes and highlights) and marginalia (additional media in articles' margins) make research convenient. Text is as important as image in informing the reader, in addition to occasional moving media and audio. The eye icon opens accessibility settings.

Queering Vietnam

2022

A visualization of messages left in Vietnam on the Queering the Map website. The non-hierarchical approach was the best fit for organizing tones and feelings. The themes are what the stories are about, and the moods describe the authors' feelings. A darker color corresponds to higher numbers of messages with the same theme or mood. The right tab lists demographic data and the limitations of this data collection.

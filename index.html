Rebranding for Le Studio Anthost

Brooklyn, NY, 2024

Le Studio Anthost needed a cohesive visual identity. The new identity reflects the Studio’s modern, minimalist approach to a mythical grandeur and bold elegance.


I established the identity across three platforms: the website, the catalogue (and other print/pdf formats like press kits, promotional brochures), and social media. The same vision is portrayed with a different tone for different audiences.

Designs for Stephani Home & Decoration

Warsaw, Poland, 2023

I worked closely with the founder of Stephani to define the brand’s identity and strategy during its early formation. I directed the creation of the online store with a developer and designer, planning features such as a blog and a unique category system tailored to the product specifications. I also directed photoshoots for website and social content and designed collateral materials, including product cards and packaging.

Manifesto for Utopia

2024

What does ChatGPT think Utopia is like? The manifesto outlines the structures of utopian life and how humanity can reach it. The text is written by ChatGPT, and edited and prompted by me. I wrote the last chapter to provide context and biases of ChatGPT. Illustrations and calligraphy by me. Hand-bound with laser-engraved leather.

Mouse Moment Menu

2024, laser-engraved fluorescent acrylic

A menu of media to fill idle time, laser engraved on fluorescent acrylic. The menu was inspired by a TikTok trend called “mouse moment,” which turned the action of eating a meal alone into a trend.

FOLIO Magazine (sound on)

2025

FOLIO is a fictional art & culture magazine with which I reimagined the digital reading experience. I propose that websites can democratize access to information; promote more intentional, critical, and convenient reading experiences; and offer an editorial experience that complements rather than copies print.

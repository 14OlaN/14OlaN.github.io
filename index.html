Rebranding for Le Studio Anthost

Brooklyn, NY, 2024

Le Studio Anthost needed a cohesive visual identity. The new identity reflects the Studio’s modern, minimalist approach to a mythical grandeur and bold elegance.


I established the identity across three platforms: the website, the catalogue (& other print/pdf formats like press kits, promotional brochures), and social media. The same vision is portrayed with a different tone for different audiences.

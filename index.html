Manifesto for Utopia

2024

What does ChatGPT think Utopia is like? The manifesto outlines the structures of utopian life and how humanity can reach it. The text is written by ChatGPT, and edited and prompted by me. I wrote the last chapter to provide context and biases of ChatGPT. Illustrations and calligraphy by me. Hand-bound with laser-engraved leather.

Queering Vietnam

2022

A visualization of messages left in Vietnam on the Queering the Map website. The themes describe the content, and the moods describe the author's feelings. A darker color corresponds to higher numbers of messages with the same theme or mood. The right tab lists demographic data and limitations of this data collection.

Ice Twister Tongue Breaker

2024

Get knotted up in the ponderings of your life, from the casual to the intimate, at unik14.github.io. The website asks questions about different parts of life, from childhood to friendships and relationships to work to death, in no particular order. You can't hit the delete button, like in a real conversation - because you are conversing with this software, and the screen and keyboard are the interface.

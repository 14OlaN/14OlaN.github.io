Mẹ có tin số phận không? - Có!

2023

Eng: Mom, do you believe in fate? - Yes!

A hand-bound collection of my ponderings about fate, consisting of interviews, dialogues, images, and screenshots, some found and some created by me. I wanted the book to feel packaged and reflect the looseness of the research as a collection of various related topics. The coptic binding is thus exposed, and the cover, the shape of which resembles a hand holding the book, is made with cardboard and kraft paper.

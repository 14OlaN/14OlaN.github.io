Manifesto for Utopia

2024

What does ChatGPT think Utopia is like? The manifesto outlines the structures of utopian life and how humanity can reach it. The text is written by ChatGPT, and edited and prompted by me. I wrote the last chapter to provide context and biases of ChatGPT. Illustrations and calligraphy by me. Hand-bound with laser-engraved leather.

2023

Eng: Mom, do you believe in fate? - Yes!

A hand-bound collection of media about fate, consisting of interviews, dialogues, images, and screenshots, some found and some created by me. The images are printed separately and pasted in, like in old books. High and low media exist harmoniously in my expression of contemporary experiences on paper.

Queering Vietnam

2022

A visualization of messages left in Vietnam on the Queering the Map website. The non-hierarchical approach was the best fit for organizing tones and feelings. The themes are what the stories are about, and the moods describe the authors' feelings. A darker color corresponds to higher numbers of messages with the same theme or mood. The right tab lists demographic data and the limitations of this data collection.

Mouse Moment Menu

2024, laser-engraved fluorescent acrylic

Commentary on surveillance capitalism perpetuated through a TikTok trend of the Mouse Moment. The menu offers a range of media that keeps (mainly) young people constantly stimulated, especially when having a meal alone. Mouse moment is a term coined on TikTok to describe this moment of solitude. People record themselves eating alone and call it "having a mouse moment".

Ice Twister Tongue Breaker

2024

Get knotted up in the ponderings of your life, from the casual to the intimate, on my website at unik14.github.io. I'm asking questions about different parts of traditional modern life, from childhood to friendships and relationships to work to death, in no particular order. You can't hit the delete button, like in a real conversation - because you are conversing with this software, and the screen and keyboard are the interface. It is only fair for you to be aware that the computer remembers everything you write, and it is only fair for the computer that you cannot edit your response, as if you were stuttering in physical speech.

Mouse Moment Menu

2024, laser-engraved fluorescent acrylic

Pick how you will fill your every waking moment with the February Mouse Moment Menu. Mouse moment is a term coined on TikTok after a video was posted of a mouse from an animated series eating M&Ms alone to some peaceful piano. It describes a moment where you are eating alone, and users have been using the audio to record themselves eating an ordinary meal alone at home, in the college dining hall, at the park, etc. The term made me angry, because even this mundane moment where I am eating and where I am already alone and unobserved has been taken from me, dissected, and churned out to become an aesthetic and product for reproduction and mass distribution. It feels like there are few of those moments left, and we deliberately try not to be present in the world by putting in earphones or doomscrolling.

A Day At The Park

2024

A humorous, absurd, but hopeful game where you explore the world where the digital and physical merge - something parallel to our world. I comment on the dread of the contemporary world I live in, where advertising and consumption are equal to religious gospel in the search for comfort and ease, where everyone and everything want to tell me something, where trust and genuine connection is rare in an age of constant interaction. Yet, rather than reacting with only cynicism and apathy, I want to stay hopeful, loving, and unjaded. The shortened game-play is shown in the images above. The game is available here. Made with Flickgame.org.

Queering Vietnam

2022

A visualisation of messages left in Vietnam on the Queering the Map website. The themes describe the content, and the moods describe the feeling of the author. Darker color corresponds to a higher number of messages with the same theme or mood. The right tab lists demographic data and limitations of this data collection.
